## Getting Help

### Common Issues

**My listing wasn't approved**
- Check photo quality (clear, well-lit)
- Ensure all details are accurate
- IMEI must be valid and not blacklisted

**I won but seller isn't responding**
- Use platform messaging
- Report through Complaints section
- We'll mediate and help resolve

**Payment issues**
- Escrow protects both parties
- Contact support for disputes
- Refunds processed within 5-7 days

### Contact Support
- Use the Complaints section in your account
- Select appropriate category
- Provide transaction details
- We respond within 24-48 hours

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my phone's IMEI safe?**
A: Yes, IMEI is encrypted and only visible to admins for verification.

**Q: Can I cancel my bid?**
A: Bids cannot be cancelled once placed. Bid responsibly.

**Q: How long do auctions last?**
A: Default is 7 days, but sellers can accept bids anytime.

**Q: What if the phone doesn't match the listing?**
A: Report immediately. Escrow protects your payment until verification.

**Q: Can I negotiate outside the platform?**
A: No, all transactions must go through PhoneBid for your protection.

**Q: How do I know if a seller is trustworthy?**
A: Check their profile, past sales, and verification status.
